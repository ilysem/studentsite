Services

MOBILE NOTARY

  • A mobile notary is a notary public who travels to clients to verify signatures on important documents. They help ensure documents are signed correctly and legally.

T.O.A

  • A temporary office assistant is someone who helps with daily tasks in an office, like organizing files, answering phones, and scheduling meetings, to keep things running smoothly.

ERRANDS

  • An errand service helps people with tasks like shopping, deliveries, and pick- ups, saving time and making life easier.

ORGANIZATION

  • Arrange, declutter, and manage spaces or tasks efficiently. This includes, office setup, scheduling, and streamlining daily operations for better productivity and convenience.

LEGAL FILING

  • Legal filing is the process of submitting off-i cial documents to a court or government office as part of a legal process. It ensures that a case, request, or record is properly recorded and processed.

PERMIT RUNNING

  • Permit running is the process of getting official permission to do something, like building, running a business, or hosting an event. It involves applying, meeting require- ments, and getting approval.
